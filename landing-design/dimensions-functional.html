Dimension 01

Self-Awareness

Self-awareness is the foundation of effective leadership. It's about understanding your strengths, weaknesses, values, and how your behavior impacts others. Leaders with high self-awareness can identify their blind spots, recognize their emotional triggers, and make conscious choices about how they show up.

This dimension includes understanding your leadership style, being open to feedback, and continuously reflecting on your actions and decisions.

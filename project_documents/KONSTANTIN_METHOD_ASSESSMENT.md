## Assessment Overview

### Three Assessment Types

**1. Hook Assessment (Free)**
- 12 questions (4 per territory)
- 5-minute completion time
- Entry point for new users
- Provides snapshot scores and identifies top strength + biggest gap

**2. Baseline Assessment (Paid)**
- 100 questions across 18 sub-dimensions
- Staged delivery: 30q â†’ 60q â†’ 100q
- Comprehensive leadership profile
- Enables weekly tracking and personalized recommendations

**3. Weekly Check-Ins**
- 3 questions per week (user-selected focus areas)
- Same 3 questions repeated for 12 weeks
- Quarterly rotation of focus areas
- Builds trend data and tracks behavioral change

## Hook Assessment (12 Questions)

**Purpose:** Free entry point, creates "aha" moment, drives signup

**Time:** ~5 minutes

**Scoring:** 4 points per question, 48 points total (16 per territory)

---

### LEADING YOURSELF (4 questions)

**Q1. Energy Management**
In the last week, how many hours did you spend on work that only you could do (not meetings, emails, or fire-fighting)?
- 1: 0-2 hours (I'm drowning)
- 2: 3-5 hours (I'm surviving)
- 3: 6-10 hours (I'm managing)
- 4: 10+ hours (I'm thriving)

**Q2. Self-Awareness**
When was the last time you said "I don't know" in front of your team?
- 1: Can't remember (I'm the expert)
- 2: More than a month ago
- 3: In the last 2 weeks
- 4: This week (I model vulnerability)

**Q3. Above the Line**
When something goes wrong, what's your first internal reaction?
- 1: "Who screwed this up?" (Blame)
- 2: "Why does this always happen?" (Victim)
- 3: "I'll fix it myself" (Hero)
- 4: "What did I miss? What can we learn?" (Curiosity)

**Q4. Contemplative Practice**
Do you have a daily practice that creates space for stillness (meditation, journaling, walking)?
- 1: No, I don't have time
- 2: I try, but it's sporadic
- 3: Yes, a few times a week
- 4: Yes, it's non-negotiable

## Analysis Frameworks

### Pattern Recognition (AI-Powered)

**When analyzing weekly check-in data, look for:**

1. **Correlation patterns:**
   - Does deep work hours correlate with stress zone?
   - When commitments slip, does psychological safety drop?
   - Do strategy reviews happen when time ON > time IN?

2. **Threshold effects:**
   - Is there a minimum deep work threshold where performance spikes?
   - Does missing weekly tactical meetings predict team health decline?
   - Is there a stress ceiling where everything breaks?

3. **Lag effects:**
   - Do contemplative practice gaps show up as reactivity 2 weeks later?
   - Does commitment-keeping impact trust scores with delay?
   - Do culture-shaping actions take 4+ weeks to show impact?

4. **Day-of-week patterns:**
   - Which days have highest/lowest scores?
   - Are Mondays consistently different from Fridays?
   - Is there a mid-week dip or peak?

5. **Seasonal patterns:**
   - Do scores dip in Q4 (holiday stress)?
   - Are there monthly patterns (month-end crunch)?
   - Do offsites create temporary spikes?

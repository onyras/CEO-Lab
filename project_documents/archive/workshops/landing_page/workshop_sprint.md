## HOW THIS DIFFERS FROM FULL WORKSHOP

**Full Workshop (3-4 hrs):**
- Deep exploration of every principle
- Write all copy in full
- Extensive examples and rationale
- Complete competitive analysis

**Sprint Workshop (15-30 min):**
- Hit all key decision points
- Bullet point answers
- Quick directional choices
- Foundation to expand from

**When to use Sprint:**
- Need quick direction
- Initial strategy session
- Rapid prototyping
- Decision-making framework

**When to use Full:**
- Need finished copy
- Want comprehensive thinking
- Exploring new territory deeply
- Building final deliverable

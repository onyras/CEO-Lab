## Image Style Guidelines

**Brand Alignment:**
- Use NK brand colors where possible: #F7F3ED (off-white), #7FABC8 (blue), #A6BEA4 (green)
- Clean, professional, minimal aesthetic
- Avoid cheesy stock photos
- Consider illustrations or abstract imagery over literal photos

**Technical Requirements:**
- Web-optimized (compressed for fast loading)
- Min resolution: 72dpi for web
- Consistent aspect ratios per section

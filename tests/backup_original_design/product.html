Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from reading leadership books?

Books give you ideas. CEO Lab gives you data about yourself. You don't just learn what good leadership looks like -- you measure where you actually stand. Then you track whether you're improving or just reading more.

What assessments do I take first?

You start with 5 core diagnostics: Zone of Genius, 5 Drivers, CEO Test, Trust Formula, and the Culture Diagnostic. These create your leadership baseline. Then you retake them monthly, quarterly, or yearly to track your evolution.

What do the AI reports actually tell me?

Every month, AI analyzes your full assessment history. It spots patterns across time -- what's improving, what's stuck, what's getting worse. You get one focused recommendation: the single highest-leverage thing to work on next.

I'm already working with an executive coach. Do I need this?

CEO Lab makes coaching better. Your coach gets better data. Your sessions become more focused. You have metrics to discuss instead of impressions. Most members say it's the best pre-work they've ever brought to a coaching conversation.

How much time does this take?

Initial setup is about 90 minutes to complete your core assessments. After that, most members spend 20-30 minutes per month on reassessments and reviewing their AI reports. The Beautiful Mind app takes 5 minutes daily if you choose to use it.

What if I'm not a CEO?

CEO Lab is for anyone in a leadership role -- founders, executives, VPs, directors. If you're responsible for decisions that affect others and want to get better at leading, this works for you. The assessments measure universal leadership capabilities.

Can my team use this?

Each membership is individual. For team deployments, contact us directly. We offer team packages where you can see aggregated (anonymous) culture data while each leader has their private dashboard.

What if I cancel?

One click from your dashboard. You keep access until the end of your billing period. Your data stays yours -- you can export everything. No guilt trips. No retention calls. We're not interested in keeping people who don't find it valuable.

Is my data private?

Your assessment results are yours alone. We never share individual data with anyone -- not employers, not investors, not anyone. The only person who sees your results is you (and your AI analysis engine).

What's the refund policy?

If you're not satisfied within your first 30 days, email us for a full refund. No questions asked. We'd rather have happy members than unhappy ones.
